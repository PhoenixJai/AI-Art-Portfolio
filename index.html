About

This portfolio showcases AI-assisted character art refined through manual editing and concept development. Most pieces reinterpret characters from modern Webtoon series, emphasizing lighting, texture, and emotional tone. Each artwork is selected, retouched, and color-graded to achieve a cohesive, high-quality finish.

The collection continues to expand into additional IPs and mediums, exploring how AI tools can support creative reinterpretation, storytelling, and cross-media design.
